Private Limited Company Registration Procedure

  • Make an application by giving your details in the form, soon our executive will contact you and ask  for your confirmation.
  • With your confirmation, you will be asked to provide the set of documents required.
  • After your successful submission of documents, we will start the Private Limited Company Registration Procedure.
  • You can contact us for tracking your private limited company registration status.
  • Within 10-20 days you will get your private company registered.

Steps to Register

  • For Private Limited Company registration first you need to apply for a Director Identification Number i.e. DIN. All the existing and intending Directors have to obtain DIN within the prescribed time-frame as notified.
  • After that, you would need to obtain your Digital Signature certificate. Get DSC at home by registering here.
  • Then, you need to get the company name approved by the ministry.
  • And once the company name is approved, you can register the company by filing the incorporation form depending on the type of company.
  • INC 29 integrates this entire process into one form.
